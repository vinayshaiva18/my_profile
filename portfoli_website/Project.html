Mini project: Wireless Audio transmission

Technology Used: Free - space optical communication utilizes light propagating in free space to wirelessly transmit data.

Modulation: Electrical data signals are converted into optical signals using a modulator

Transmission: The modulated laser beams are transmitted through the air

Reception: At the receiving end ,the optical signal is detected and converted back into electrical data.
